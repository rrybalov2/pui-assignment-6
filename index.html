Introducing myGlu

A simple dashboard for diabetics to see their status for activities in relation to their blood sugar level.




How can diabetics correlate all of their disparate data points  in order to keep their blood sugar steady?

Diabetics need to keep track of their daily activity to effectively manage their diabetes. They use a wide variety of standalone apps , but none of them connect with each other.


More Common Than You Think




myGlu's dashboard helps diabetics view this food intake and excersize activity on a single chart

You can easily see how your actions throughout the day impact your blood sugar levels. Using myGlu's recommendations, you can now make instant and informed decisions about how you control your day.
